Hunting Energy - Cross-Regional Win with Multiple Solutions
The Hunting Energy win is an example of one of the teaming successes between our GEAR and APAC sellers.

Hunting Energy -- an international energy company that provides products and services to the upstream oil and gas industry -- with a global footprint can meet virtually any of their customer requirements with a full range of products and services.

In addition to purchasing an MPLS network where additional resources are required to manage their global connectivity, Hunting wanted to migrate their managed hosting from their previous 3rd party provider to CenturyLink. Blake Irwin and his team worked with Hunting’s CEO from their Houston office to identify their needs in the APAC region. Using the Global Sales Program, Blake leveraged Shantini’s knowledge and insights of the region to understand the various country’s technical and legal requirements.
 
Due to time differences, the two teams were able to provide the follow-the-sun sales model. Shantini worked with the in-region client where she provided information in a timely manner. With her presence locally, she was able to set expectations, managed deadlines and moved the deal forward.
